Hu Wang
I am a research scientist working with Prof. Mohammad Yaqub at MBZUAI (Ranked 11th in AI globally). Previously, I worked as a research fellow at AIML, Adelaide University with Prof. Gustavo Carneiro. I received my Ph.D. degree advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen and Prof. Qi Wu at Adelaide University. Before that, I worked with Prof. Steven Hoi (the author of BLIP and BLIP2) on at LARC cofounded by SMU and CMU. Our Imagendo work was awarded the Eureka Prize (Australia premier national science award). Research interests: Multi-modal Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Medical AI, AI Security, etc.
