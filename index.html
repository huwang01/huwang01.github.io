Hu Wang
I am a research scientist working with Prof. Mohammad Yaqub at MBZUAI (Ranked 14th in AI globally). Previously, I worked as a research fellow at AIML, Adelaide University with Prof. Gustavo Carneiro. I received my Ph.D. degree advised by Prof. Chunhua Shen and Prof. Qi Wu at Adelaide University. Before that, I worked with Prof. Steven Hoi on at SMU, LARC cofounded by CMU. In August 2023, our Imagendo work was awarded the Eureka Prize (Australia premier national science award). Research interests: Medical AI, Multi-modal Learning, Reinforcement Learning, AI Security, etc.
